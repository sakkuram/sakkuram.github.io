ABOUT

My name is Oleksandra Miziuk and I am a freelance designer with a passion for creating innovative
and visually stunning designs. Having completed art school, I have honed my skills and have over
a year of experience in web design, logo design, and branding work.
As a web designer, I have a keen eye for aesthetics and a deep understanding of user experience
design. I can take your website from concept to completion, ensuring that it is both visually
appealing and easy to navigate.
When it comes to logo design, I believe that a great logo is more than just a pretty picture - it is
the embodiment of a brand's identity. I work closely with my clients to understand their brand's
mission and values, and use this knowledge to create logos that are not only visually appealing,
but also meaningful and memorable.
Finally, as a branding specialist, I understand the importance of consistency and cohesiveness in a
brand's identity. From color schemes to typography, I can create a comprehensive branding
strategy that will help your brand stand out in a crowded marketplace.
One of my biggest strengths is my ability to work quickly without sacrificing quality. I believe
that creativity and efficiency can go hand in hand, and I take pride in delivering projects that
exceed my clients' expectations.

"Cat's House" is a cozy cafe located in the heart of the city,
known for its unique and charming atmosphere. The cafe is home
to several adorable and friendly cats, who roam freely around
the cafe and interact with the customers. It's a perfect spot for
cat lovers to relax, unwind, and enjoy the company of these
furry felines.

The idea for "Cat's House" was born out of the owners' love for cats and the desire to create
a unique cafe experience. The owners, a couple named maria and sergey, had always been
passionate about cats and had several of their own. They also loved coffee and the idea of
creating a space where people could relax and enjoy a good cup of coffee while being
surrounded by cats.
